Routes

Users

Method Route Description
GET "/users/:id" Gets a single user.
POST "/users/register" Registers a user.
POST "/users/login" Logs in a user and generates a token.
GET "/users/verify" Decrypts the token to gain access to the user's details.
DELETE "/users/:id" Deletes a specific user.
PUT "/users/:id" Edits a user.

Cart

Method Route Description
GET "/users/:id/cart"
POST "/users/:id/cart" Gets all items inside of the cart.
DELETE "/users/:id/cart/:id" Removes an item from cart.
PATCH "/users/:id/cart/:id" Alters the quantity of the product in the table.
DELETE "/users/:id/cart" Clears all items from the cart.

Products

Method Route Description
GET "/products" Displays all products.
GET "/products/:id" Displays a single product with the specific id.

Business

Method Route Description
GET "/business/" Gets all business.
GET "/business/:ID" Gets a single business.
POST "/business/register" Registers a business.
POST "/business/login" Log's in a business and generates a token.
GET "/business/business/verify" Decrypts the token to gain access to the business information.
DELETE "/business/:id" Deletes a business
PUT "/business/:id" Edits the details of a business.
GET "/business/products" Gets all products with a similiar business id to the business.
GET "/business/products/:id" Gets a specific product.
POST "/business/products/" Adds a new product to website.
DELETE "/business/products/:id Deletes a product from the array.
PUT "/business/products/:id" Allows the business to edit a product.
